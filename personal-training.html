Good to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of personal training programs do you offer?

We offer programs for every level and goal: one-on-one training, sports performance, weight loss & body composition, small group training (2–4 people), and online programs. Whether you want to build strength, lose weight, improve performance, or move better, there's a fit for you.

How are training programs customized to my goals?

It starts with an assessment of your strength, endurance, mobility, body composition, and injury history. From there your trainer designs a program with the right exercise selection, progressive overload, and periodization for your goals — then adjusts it around your schedule, recovery, and progress over time.

How do I get started with a trainer?

Reach out through this page (or by phone or in person) and book your 1-hour evaluation. We assess your fitness and define your goals, pair you with the certified trainer best suited to you, and build a customized program. Then you choose a schedule — mornings, evenings, or weekends — and start training.

What are the pricing and package options?

Every client begins with a one-time evaluation, then trains in a full package: 6-Pack ($110/session), 12-Pack ($105/session), 24-Pack ($100/session), or 48-Pack ($95/session) — each bundling public membership. We also offer an On/Off Campus plan ($375/month) for experienced lifters and online training as our most affordable option. Packages are sold in full; individual sessions aren't available.

Do sessions expire? What's the cancellation policy?

Training sessions never expire, though the included public access holds to its listed time frame. Packages are non-refundable and non-transferable. For individual sessions, please give at least 24 hours' notice to cancel or reschedule — late cancellations or no-shows forfeit the session. If your trainer cancels, you're rescheduled at no penalty.

Where are you and what are your hours?

We're at 8356 Musick Memorial Dr, Brentwood, MO 63144. Staffed hours: Mon–Thu 6 AM–7 PM, Fri 6 AM–5 PM, Sat 6 AM–2 PM, Sun 8 AM–2 PM. Members have 24/7 keycard access, 365 days a year.
